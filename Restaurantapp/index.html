Paradiso del Cibo

Paradiso del Cibo was started in 1671 by the great Lord Manchester who loved food. Its legacy was passed on to its generations and now it stands as the best restaurant in the whole universe. It has been rated the best restaurant by "The Forbes" in 2019.This place is the most awesome place to haveyour dinner with your friends, eating the classic Chicken wingsand French Fries, or date with a mistletoe over your head and a classic violin music with extravagant French Cuisine.This place has got all sorts of drinks and beverages you need for any time. This place has got a 500 year old scotch bottle still to be openedin its 500th year anniversary.
